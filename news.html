Our Mission

To promote and support local artisans by providing a platform that showcases their unique handmade crafts. To inspire appreciation for Filipino craftsmanship through storytelling behind the crafts and sustainable local merchandise. Likha aims to preserve Filipino craftsmanship, support sustainable livelihood, and connect communities through creativity and culture.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant community where people connect through making, becoming a known resource for both inspiring handmade finished goods and fostering new crafters.
